GEMSBOK


Scientific Name: Oryx gazella

Family: Bovidae

Habitat: Arid regions, including deserts and savannahs in Southern Africa


The gemsbok, also known as the South African oryx, is a large antelope well-adapted to life in dry, harsh climates. With long, spear-like horns and bold facial markings, these majestic animals are known for their resilience. They can survive extreme heat and go for long periods without water, extracting moisture from their food.
