LOST FOREST


"A hidden world of discovery and relaxation!"

Wander into the Lost Forest, a lush, shaded haven where adventure meets tranquility. This immersive area is home to some of the zoo’s most elusive and fascinating creatures all nestled beneath a canopy of greenery. But it’s not all mystery and mist—

The Lost Forest also offers peaceful picnic spots where families and friends can unwind, enjoy their packed meals, and take a quiet break surrounded by nature. Whether you’re exploring hidden paths or simply soaking in the serenity, the Lost Forest is the perfect place to pause, refresh, and reconnect with the wild.


WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

The lost Forest is located in the east of the zoo, beyond the monkey trail
