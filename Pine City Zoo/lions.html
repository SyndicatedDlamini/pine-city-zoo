LIONS


Scientific Name: Panthera leo

Family: Felidae

Habitat: Grasslands, savannahs, and open woodlands of Africa; small population in India (Asiatic Lion)


Lions are powerful big cats and the only species of cats that live in social groups called prides. A pride can consist of up to 30 lions, mostly related females and their cubs, protected by a coalition of males.

Known for their iconic manes, only male lions grow them, which signal age and dominance. Lions hunt in groups and rely on cooperation to take down large prey like wildebeests and zebras.

Conservation Status: Vulnerable
