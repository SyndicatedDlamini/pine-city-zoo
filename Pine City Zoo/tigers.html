TIGERS


cientific Name: Panthera tigris

Family:Felidae

Habitat:Dense forests, grasslands, and mangrove swamps across Asia


Tigers are the largest of the big cats and are known for their power, agility, and striking appearance. Their bold stripes provide excellent camouflage in dense vegetation. Tigers are solitary and territorial, marking their domain with scent and scratches.

There are several subspecies of tigers, including the Bengal, Siberian, and Sumatran tigers. Each has adapted to its specific environment, from snowy forests to tropical jungles.

Conservation Status:Endangered
