INSECT HOUSE


"A world of wonders, six legs at a time!"

Step into the Insect House, one of the largest and most diverse insect collections in the world. From dazzling butterflies and leaf-mimicking stick insects to armored beetles and busy ants, this immersive exhibit showcases creepy crawlies of every shape, size, and color from across the globe.

Walk through tropical environments buzzing with life, peer into fascinating micro-habitats, and discover the vital role insects play in ecosystems—from pollination to decomposition. Whether you’re fascinated or fearless, the Insect House offers a fun and educational experience for visitors of all ages.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

You will find the insect house on the eastern side of the zoo,next to the monkey Trail.
